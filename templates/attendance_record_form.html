Retrieve Attendance Record

Click on the icon at right side of the form to view the instructions before filling out the form

In the field "Retrieve Attendance According to Type" there are four options for that what parameters should you choose are mentioned below:

  • View dates when the student was present / absent: Select the specific student and the status.
  • View the attendance status of a student for a particular date: Enter the student ID and select the date.
  • View attendance of all the students for a particular date: Select the date to view the attendance.
  • View monthly attendance data for all the students: Select the month and year.
